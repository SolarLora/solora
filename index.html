The principles
▸ Works forever without us. No server dependency. No account required.
▸ Your Bitcoin address is stored only on your device. We never see it.
▸ Open source firmware. Audit every line. Fork it. Improve it.
▸ If we disappeared tomorrow your device keeps mining forever.
▸ Relay tally builds toward Solora coin — earned by network work, not bought.
